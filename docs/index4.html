Oatmeal Cookies

    Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 155g of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 50g of instant Oatmeal

Procedures

  1. Put room temperature butter, sugar and salt in a mixing bowl and mix
  2. Add egg and vanilla extract then mix
  3. Add flour and baking soda. Mix
  4. Add the oats
  5. Mix the ingredients until it makes a dough like mixture
  6. Roll it into a log shape and freeze it for 30 mins. Slice and bake
  7. Airfry it for 15 mins in 180 degrees celcius
