Sweet & Sour Shrimp

    Ingredients:
  • Shrimp (any preferred amount)
  • Jufran Sauce/Ketchup
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Worcetershire Sauce

Procedures

  1. Ready your pan with oil. When the pan is hot put the onions and garlic
  2. Saute the onions and garlic for about 3-5 mins
  3. When the onions and garlic are done put the shrimp. Cook the shrimp until the shrimp turns orange
  4. After cooking the shrimp, add the ketchup or the Jufran sauce
  5. Add a bit of salt and Worcetershire Sauce
  6. Mix the ingredients together for about 5-6 mins
  7. Serve and eat
