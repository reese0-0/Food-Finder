Cheesy Nachos

    Ingredients:
  • Nachos (any preferred amount)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Groud Pork
  • Taco Powder
  • Cheese

Procedures

  1. Ready your pan with oil. When the pan is hot put the ground pork
  2. When the ground pork turns to a brownish color put taco powder and mix it well
  3. Ready your air fryer. Put the nachos first then sprinkle it with onion, tomatoes, cheese and ground pork
  4. Do a second layer. repeat the same process in step 3
  5. Optional: You can do as many layers as you want
  6. After layering the ingredients put it in the air fryer and set it to 180 degrees celcius for about 15 mins
  7. Serve and eat
